
# Golf Rivals Brand Manual

## Brand Concept

Golf Rivals is a premium golf rivalry app for live matches between friends, hole-by-hole stakes, skins, mulligans, reverse mulligans, carried pots and team balances.

Main tagline:
Every Hole Has a Price.

Secondary tagline:
Play smart. Win the hole.

## Brand Personality

- Premium
- Competitive
- Strategic
- Clear
- Sport-driven
- Elegant
- Mobile-first
- Useful on the course

## Official Color Palette

- Midnight Fairway: #0F2D28
- Carbon Black: #181B1E
- Sand White: #EFE8DA
- Victory Gold: #C6A15B
- Turf Accent: #5FA36A
- Danger / Penalty: #C95C4A
- Warning / Carry: #D9A441
- Muted Border: #2E3A36

## Color Usage

- Victory Gold is used for money, pot values, main CTAs and premium highlights.
- Turf Accent is used for live status, success states, winners and active states.
- Danger is used for penalties, errors, unavailable actions or negative balances.
- Warning is used for carry, pending actions or attention states.
- Sand White is used for primary text.
- Carbon Black and Midnight Fairway are used for backgrounds, cards and dark surfaces.
- Muted Border is used for subtle divisions, cards and interface structure.

## UI Principles

- The app must feel premium, dark, competitive and practical.
- The live game screen must be readable outdoors.
- Buttons must be large and easy to tap.
- Money values must be highly visible.
- Cards should use subtle borders, not heavy decoration.
- Gold should be used with restraint.
- Green should clearly communicate live, success or advantage.
- Red should only be used when something is negative, blocked or risky.

## What To Avoid

- Casino look
- Neon colors
- Generic fantasy sports style
- Old-fashioned golf club clichés
- Excessive decoration
- Tiny text
- Low contrast
- Random team colors disconnected from the brand

## Logo

Current SVG:
`/public/brand-logo.svg`

Usage:
- Keep it clean and centered when used as the main identity.
- Use simplified versions for app icon, favicon and small navigation areas.
- Avoid overly detailed versions at small sizes.

## Summary

Golf Rivals should feel like a premium competitive golf tool.

Dark, sharp, clear and strategic.

Every hole matters.
Every decision has value.
Every hole has a price.
